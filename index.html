🏴‍☠️ About Me

My name is Birat Shiwakoti, a pirate of the sea whose name echoes across the waves. I don’t sail for rules, I sail for adventure, power, and destiny. The world calls pirates criminals — I call them cowards who fear freedom.

From the darkest oceans to the brightest horizons, I chase legends and create my own. My crew follows me not because they must, but because they believe in the storm I bring.

⚡ Special Ability

I possess a rare and terrifying power that makes me different from every other pirate sailing the seas.

Devil Fruit Copy Ability:
I can copy the powers of any other Devil Fruit user and use their abilities for 30 minutes at full strength.

For half an hour, I become a living nightmare — fire, lightning, ice, darkness, beasts, or gods. When time ends, the borrowed power fades... but the legend remains.

🌊 Pirate Creed

I fear no storm, no enemy, and no empire. The sea is my home, the sky is my map, and freedom is the treasure I hunt.

My goal is simple: to become the pirate whose name is written in history forever.
